About Me

I'm a finance enthusiast, a web and mobile developer, life learner, sports fan, and optimistic DO'er. I'm very casual and I pride myself on an easy-going and honest personality. I work hard to keep a strong work-ethic, open mind, and aspire to make my own Jobsian "dent in the world." I don't believe in intelligence, I believe in grit and creativity.
